Background

I am a third year IT Cybersecurity major at Wright State University my goal is to enter the field of Digital Forensics. Alongside my studies, I have taken on multiple leadership roles, working as a Resident Assistant and an Admissions Ambassador. I'm also holding positions as Co-President for the National Society of Black Engineers and Secretary of Engineers Without Borders. I'm also an active member of Black Men On The Move. Through my academic and extracurricular commitments, I'm committed to using my technical skills and leadership experience, contribute to the field of digital forensics while supporting my community.

Over the years, I have developed strong skills in problem-solving and research, which are indispensable for excelling in the cybersecurity field. These abilities have enabled me to approach challenges methodically and devise innovative solutions. My projects serve as a testament to my dedication to continuous learning and staying ahead in the ever-evolving technology landscape. Whether collaborating with a team or working independently, I am driven by a passion for finding creative ways to secure and optimize systems in the digital age.

Feel free to explore my work and interests, register for updates, and connect with me to discuss potential collaborations.
